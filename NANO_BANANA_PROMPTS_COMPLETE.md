## 📚 Nano Banana Pro Best Practices (Research-Based)

### ✅ Golden Rules from Official Google Guide

1. **Use natural, full-sentence prompts** — Not keyword lists
2. **Specify subject + setting + lighting + style + composition** — Complete descriptions
3. **Lock visual elements across sequences** — Character consistency
4. **Generate 3-5 variations** — Selection is faster than iteration
5. **Use multimodal inputs** — Upload sketches, brand assets, references
6. **Specify resolution tier** — 1024px for iteration, 4K for final

### 📐 Prompt Formula (Proven Structure)

```
[Design Type] for [Brand/Product]
**Subject:** [What appears in the image]
**Setting/Background:** [Environment or backdrop]
**Style:** [Visual style, mood, aesthetic]
**Colors:** [Specific hex codes or color names]
**Lighting:** [Direction, quality, time of day]
**Composition:** [Framing, placement, rule of thirds]
**Text Elements:** [Any text to render, font style]
**Technical:** [Format, resolution, aspect ratio]
**Brand Context:** [Target audience, personality, region]
```

### 🎯 Character Consistency (FENNEC Mascot)

**Critical FENNEC Features (MUST include in every prompt):**

**DO NOT:**
- ❌ Red/orange fur (that's a red fox)
