# FAZUMI Brand Voice Kit v1
**Status:** Active
**Date:** 2026-03-03

---

## Goal
Apply a consistent brand voice across FAZUMI user-facing copy to support conversion, retention, and trust for busy GCC parents.

## Audience
- Bilingual Arabic and English parents with K-6 children
- MENA and GCC context
- High sensitivity to accuracy, privacy, and price clarity

## Voice
- Premium-minimal concierge friend
- Calm, clear, decisive
- Matter-of-fact, respectful, warm-minimal

## Promise
- Know what matters in 10 seconds

## Writing Rules

### Do
- Start with the outcome
- Lead with actions and deadlines
- Use short sentences and low punctuation
- Reassure calmly with factual language

### Do Not
- Use guilt language
- Overpromise with terms like "guaranteed" or "never miss"
- Use hype language like "revolutionary" or "magic AI"
- Use slang like "الزبدة"

## Priority Checklist
- Priority 1: Landing hero headline, subtitle, CTA, and trust line
- Priority 2: How It Works title and step copy
- Priority 3: Pricing page descriptions and refund note
- Priority 4: FAQ trust and accuracy calibration
- Priority 5: About page founder story and factual metrics
- Priority 6: Summarize page titles, loading, errors, and limits copy
- Priority 7: Contact page and contact form microcopy
- Priority 8: Dashboard empty, loading, and delete states
- Priority 9: Reusable trust block component and landing usage
- Priority 10: Footer trust disclaimer

## This Codex Slice
- Implement only Priority 1 to Priority 3
- Preserve existing structure and styling unless a copy change requires a small markup adjustment

## Verification
- `pnpm lint`
- `pnpm typecheck`
- Manual landing review in English and Arabic
- Ensure no `.env*` files are staged
